Menlo Atherton Adult Women's Soccer League

The Menlo Atherton Adult Soccer League (MAASL) is a non-profit soccer league for women 23 years and older. We are based in beautiful Menlo Park, California and welcome players of all ability levels. The emphasis of our league is on getting fit and having fun.

We play two season per year: mid March through mid-May, and September through mid-November. Game days are Saturday and Monday, Tuesday, or Thursday evenings depending on field assignments set by the City of Menlo Park. All games are officiated by paid referees.

IMPORTANT! To stay informed about schedule changes, rain-outs, special events, application due dates, etc., all participants should subscribe to MAASL's Google Group (use the form below).
