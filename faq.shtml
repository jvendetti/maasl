  How do I know if my game is rained out?
  • Your team coordinator will notify you via email.
  • An announcement is posted on the MAASL mailing list.
  • An announcement is posted on the MAASL Facebook page.
  • The Menlo Park rainout hotline (650.330.2590) lists field closures.
  Do you make up rainouts?
No, unfortunately due to the high demand of field space we are unable to make up games.
